About

Shrutkirti Ranjeet Sawant is an acclaimed Indian actress working in Marathi films, TV shows, and television serials. With over a decade of experience in theatre, she has established herself as a versatile performer in the Marathi entertainment industry. Known for her compelling performances and dedication to her craft, she has won the hearts of audiences across Maharashtra.

Born: 11th November 1996
Height: 5'4" (1.63 m)
Education: BA in Dramatics, Vivekanand College
